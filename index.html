About The Event

About The Event

Welcome to a milestone occasion in the history of our esteemed institute! We are delighted to announce an unprecedented gathering that celebrates the legacy and accomplishments of our Electrical Engineering department alumni over the past four decades.
This unique event marks the first-ever union of Electrical Engineering alumni, spanning 40 years of innovation, excellence along with professors and students. Designed to be an immersive experience, our gathering promises a blend of insightful keynote sessions, engaging networking opportunities, stimulating industry discussions, and a showcase of groundbreaking innovations followed by culinary delight.
